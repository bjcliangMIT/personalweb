Research

Projects and agenda

Job market paper

Data-Making Fans and Platform Governance

Studies how fan labor, informal data practices, and platform governance co-evolve in digitally mediated communities, with implications for platform strategy, governance design, and organizational adaptation.

Presented at ASA 2025, INAS 2025, EGOS 2025, and the International System Dynamics Conference 2025

Candidacy paper

From Opinion Dynamics to Collective Action

Examines how asymmetric tolerance can turn disagreement into durable polarization, linking micro-level opinion dynamics to collective action. Supervised by Ezra Zuckerman and Ray Reagans at MIT.

Presented at PaCSS 2022, ASA 2023, and INAS 2023

Working paper

From Composition to Conduct in the Boardroom

A dynamic NK model of board behavior, bringing simulation methods to questions of board composition, conduct, and decision-making.
